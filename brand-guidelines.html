Shark Gaming Brand Guidelines

These guidelines are designed to help everyone communicate our brand with confidence and consistency.

Please use this as a reference whenever you create an Shark Gaming experience - from product to packaging to communications.

Correct Use of the Logo Test

Manipulation

Do not change, distort, recolor, or reconfigure the Shark Gaming logo.

And do not edit any fonts.

  • Incorrect

    Do not colorize the logo.

  • Incorrect

    Do not distort, flip, skew or rotate the logo.

  • Incorrect

    Do not change the font.

Spacing

Follow this simple guideline to make sure that the logo have the correct breathing room all around.

  • Correct

    No matter the size of the logo, make sure that the 'S' is used as correct minimum of spacing all around.

  • Incorrect

    No spacing have been applied. A minimum spacing of the 'S' needs to be applied.
